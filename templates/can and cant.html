WHO CAN \ WHO CAN'T?

Eligible for blood donation:

  • Individuals in good health and feeling well on the day of donation.

  • Typically,donors should be 17 years or older,and meet the minimum weight requirement (usually around 110 pounds or 50 kilograms).
  • Having a normal blood pressure and pulse rate.
  • Individuals must follow a healthy diet to donate blood.

Not eligible for blood donation:

  • People with certain medical conditions or illnesses.

  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women.

  • Those who've recently traveled to certain regions.

  • If you have recently had a tattoo or body piercing, you cannot donate for 6 months from the date of the procedure.
