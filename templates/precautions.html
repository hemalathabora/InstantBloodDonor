What to Do Before, During and After Your Blood Donation

Before Donation

Ensure you are eligible:
~Check if you meet the minimum age and weight requirements,are in good health,and haven't donated blood too recently....
Ensure a nutritious meal:
~Eat a balanced meal before donating to maintain blood sugar levels.
Hydrate well:
~Drink plenty of fluids before donation to stay hydrated.

During Donation

Relax and stay calm:
~Calm during the process to reduce stress and prevent complications.
Inform the staff of any discomfort:
~Let the medical staff know if you feel dizzy or unwell during donation.
Avoid heavy weights:
~Avoid lifting heavy weights with the donation arm or participating in strenuous physical activities.

AFTER DONATION

Rest:
~Take a short rest after donating to allow your body to recover.
Avoid smoking and alcohol:
~Refrain from smoking and alcohol consumption for at least a few hours after donation.
Follow post-donation instructions:
~Adhere to any specific instructions provided by the medical staff.
